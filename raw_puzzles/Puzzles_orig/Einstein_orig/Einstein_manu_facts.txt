There are 5 houses (in a row) in 5 different styles: Victorian, Colonial, Modern, Wooden, and Palace-Style.
In each house there lives a person of a different profession: Pilot, Artst, Engineer, Doctor, or Policeman.
These 5 owners each eat a certain type of fruit: Apples, Bananas, Oranges, Grapes, or Melons.
They also play a cerntain musical instrument: Cello, Violin, Piano, Guitar, or Harp.
Additionally, they also collect a certain type of objects: Trophies, Antique Furniture, Paintings, Coins, or Statues.
The owners DO NOT collect the same object, play the same instrument, or eat the same fruit.
